A Quick Note About Installation

The apps on this page are my own personal projects and are not available through the Google Play Store.

To install one:

  • Tap the Download APK button.
  • Open the downloaded file.
  • If Android asks for permission to install apps from your browser, choose Allow.
  • Tap Install.

Android may warn you that the application comes from outside the Play Store. That's expected. These apps were developed by me and are offered here for anyone who would like to try them.

I hope you find them useful. Enjoy!
